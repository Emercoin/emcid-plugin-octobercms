How to retrieve your credentials

  1. Go to Emercoin ID site (at https://oauth.authorizer.io/) and click Create a New App link.
  2. Enter your App Name (usually your site name) along with any other required fields and click Create App
  3. Copy your Client App ID and App Secret into the fields below.
  4. Click Edit on your App you've just created, and set the valid redirect URIs to
